Services

Advisory and engineering. One firm. One accountable structure.

Our services bring together a full institutional advisory practice and the engineering capability to deliver what we recommend. Where most firms specialize in one or the other, Genosis owns both — strategy, technology, and execution under a single engagement.

01Institutional Advisory Practice

Strategy, structure, and governance — built to hold.

A full advisory practice that operates alongside, and where appropriate independently of, our engineering capability. We work with executive leadership, ministers, agency heads, and board-level sponsors to turn strategic intent into structured programs.

Operations advisory

Operating-model design, process redesign, performance frameworks, and structured improvement across front- and back-office functions.

Organization advisory

Structural design, accountability frameworks, workforce planning, change management, and the cultural conditions for transformation to hold.

Technology & digital advisory

Technology strategy, architecture review, vendor and platform selection, program governance, and independent advisory on programs in motion.

Strategic & institutional advisory

Strategic planning, business cases, governance design, regulatory alignment, public-sector reform, and multi-party stakeholder strategies.

02Technology Development & Integration

The technology layer that makes a program operational.

Built around the institutional reality of the market — not adapted from a generic SaaS template.

Custom system development

Built around participant behavior, institutional constraints, and the integration requirements of the deployment environment.

Integration with existing infrastructure

Layers that connect with eGov, banking, healthcare, and institutional systems already in place — without replacing them.

Data architecture

Structured participant data supporting identity verification, financial access, credentialing, and institutional reporting.

Security & compliance

Architecture designed around regulatory requirements, data protection, and operational accountability.

03Implementation & Pilot Execution

We deploy, manage adoption, and stay accountable.

We don't hand implementation to a vendor and walk away — we remain accountable through the transition into operating infrastructure.

Pilot deployment

Controlled rollout at defined participant scale — typically 10,000 to start — within the regulated institutional environment.

Adoption management

Operational support during pilot and adoption: onboarding workflows, institutional training, and participant engagement.

Scale expansion

Structured expansion from pilot through adoption (250K) into infrastructure (750K+), with handover defined at each stage.

Operating model handover

Long-term responsibility transitions to the institutional owner with documentation, training, and ongoing partnership.
