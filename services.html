Services

Advisory and engineering. One firm. One accountable structure.

Our services bring together a full institutional advisory practice and the engineering capability to deliver what we recommend. Where most firms specialize in one or the other, Genosis owns both — strategy, technology, and execution under a single engagement.

Institutional advisory practice

Genosis runs a full institutional advisory practice that operates alongside, and where appropriate independently of, our engineering capability. Our advisory work spans operating-model design, organizational structure, technology strategy, and the broader strategic and policy frame within which institutional programs must hold.

We work with executive leadership, ministers, agency heads, and board-level sponsors to translate strategic intent into structured programs of work — with governance, accountability, and measurable success criteria built in from the start.

Operations advisory

Operating-model design, process review and redesign, performance measurement frameworks, cost and efficiency analysis, and structured improvement of service delivery across front-line and back-office functions.

Organization advisory

Structural design, role and accountability frameworks, workforce planning, capability assessment, change management, leadership alignment, and the cultural conditions required for transformation to hold.

Technology & digital advisory

Technology strategy, architecture review, vendor and platform selection support, program governance, and independent advisory on transformation programs already in motion.

Strategic & institutional advisory

Strategic planning, business case development, governance design, regulatory and policy alignment, public-sector reform programs, inter-agency coordination, and stakeholder engagement strategies for complex multi-party initiatives.

Technology development & integration

We develop and integrate the technology layer that makes each program operational — built around the institutional reality of the market, not adapted from a generic SaaS template.

Custom system development

Programs are built around participant behavior, institutional constraints, and integration requirements specific to the deployment environment.

Integration with existing infrastructure

Layers that connect with eGov, banking, healthcare, and institutional systems already in place — without replacing them.

Data architecture

Structured participant data that supports identity verification, financial access, credentialing, and institutional reporting.

Security & compliance

Architecture designed around regulatory requirements, institutional data protection, and operational accountability.

Implementation & pilot execution

We don't hand off implementation to a vendor and walk away. We deploy the pilot, manage adoption, and remain accountable for the transition into operating infrastructure.

Pilot deployment

Controlled rollout at defined participant scale (typically 10,000 to start) within the regulated and institutional environment.

Adoption management

Operational support during pilot and adoption phases — onboarding workflows, institutional training, participant engagement.

Scale expansion

Structured expansion from pilot through adoption (250K) into infrastructure (750K+), with institutional handover defined at each stage.

Operating model handover

Long-term operational responsibility transitions to the institutional owner with structured documentation, training, and ongoing partnership.
