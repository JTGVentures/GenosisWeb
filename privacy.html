Privacy Policy

Genosis ("we," "us") operates the website genosis.io. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Information we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the information you provide — your name, email address, and message. We do not require you to create an account or provide more than you choose to share.

How we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and communicate with you about it. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties.

How submissions are handled

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service that delivers your submission to us. Formspree handles the data according to its own privacy policy.

Analytics and cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. If we use analytics, it is a privacy-friendly, cookieless tool that collects only aggregate, anonymized traffic data and does not identify you. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Data retention

We keep contact submissions only as long as needed to respond and maintain our records, and delete them when no longer needed.

Your choices

You may request that we delete the information you submitted by contacting us at contact@genosis.io.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the "last updated" date reflects the latest version.
